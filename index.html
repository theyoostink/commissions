    About Me

    Hello, I'm Yoostink! Welcome to my commissions gallery - a place where I can showcase the artwork I commissioned.

    I like to commission art from artists whom I admire for their work, especially the ones who draw Lucina a lot. I love Lucina! I think she's a very pretty and strong-willed lady. Most of my commissions will have her as the main subject. If you're a fan of Lucina too, please consider joining the 1000+ member Lucina Fan Club Discord server using the invite link: https://discord.gg/lucina

    Unfortunately, I don't plan on accepting follower requests on Twitter. I only use my Twitter account to follow and contact artists. If you pester me with DMs or requests, I will block you. :-(

    This is a series of commissioned artwork depicting Lucina dressed in the Hooters outfit. The following are some frequently-asked questions that I've seen in the comments section of published artwork. The answers should provide information and help clear things up... or not.

    Why do you commission Lucina in the Hooters outfit?

    Hooters Lucina was an inside joke between me and an artist friend. I commissioned them to draw the first Hooters Lucina back in 2018. Now, I guess it's something we can all share. Also, it is not a fetish.

    What was the inside joke?

    Take a guess.

    How much have you spent on these commissions?

    I have a spreadsheet tracking every request I make and budget accordingly, so I know the exact numbers. But, the details are none of your business. :^)

    Why so many commissions?

    I first started with sending a Hooters Lucina request to a handful of artists. To my surprise, all of them were accepted. I thought the idea was too weird and zany to be accepted, which is why I shotgunned requests hoping for just one to be accepted. And yes, my requests have been denied for those reasons, but an overwhelming majority of artists to whom I sent requests seem to like the Hooters Lucina idea and have accepted (about 90%). After the first few were delivered, I decided to request more as I saw more artists opening up commissions - artists whom I really admire for their work. I then decided to keep this commission series going as a way to support my favorite artists. Plus, it's fun to see people's reactions.

    Why do you commission anonymously?

    It's just more fun that way - adds a sense of mystery. >:)

    Will the series ever end?

    Yes, I do plan on ending the series. I will transition to commissioning Lucina in other outfits but not as a series, and the frequency of requests will drop. I may revisit Hooters Lucina and request it here and there however.
